SPECIFIC ERROR EXAMPLES

Critical Error #1: Avenue vs Street Abbreviations

The Problem

Found across 80%+ of locations - inconsistent street type abbreviations create NAP mismatches.

Example - North York Location:

  • Google Business Profile: "123 Dufferin Avenue"
  • Weedmaps: "123 Dufferin Ave"
  • Yelp: "123 Dufferin Ave."
  • Apple Maps: "123 Dufferin Street" (WRONG)

Impact: Google sees 4 different addresses and can't verify which is correct → reduced local rankings

Critical Error #2: Business Name Chaos

4 Different Name Formats Found

  1. "Sessions Cannabis - North York" (with dash, includes Cannabis)
  2. "Sessions North York" (no dash, no Cannabis)
  3. "Sessions - North York" (dash, no Cannabis)
  4. "Sessions Cannabis" (no location identifier)

Recommendation: Standardize to Format #1 across ALL platforms

Critical Error #3: Wrong City Designations

Geographic Boundary Errors

Several locations listed with incorrect city/municipality:

  • Scarborough location listed as "Toronto" (technically correct but SEO-suboptimal)
  • Etobicoke location also listed as "Toronto" (same issue)
  • North York location sometimes "North York," sometimes "Toronto"

Why this matters: "cannabis north york" has 2,400 monthly searches. Listing as "Toronto" misses these hyper-local searches.

DUPLICATE LISTINGS PROBLEM

The Duplicate Trap

Found multiple instances where same location appears TWICE on same platform with different NAP data:

Example - North York on Google:

  • Listing #1: "Sessions Cannabis - North York" at "123 Dufferin Avenue"
  • Listing #2: "Sessions North York" at "123 Dufferin Ave"

Impact:

  • Reviews split across 2 listings (dilutes rating power)
  • Google unsure which is "real" listing (suppresses both)
  • Customers confused about which to trust
  • Fixing requires Google Business Profile support (time-consuming)

Found duplicates on: Google (3 locations), Yelp (2 locations), Weedmaps (1 location)

PRIORITIZATION FRAMEWORK

My 3-Tier Prioritization

Tier 1: Critical (Fix Weeks 1-2)

  • Wrong city/region designations (5-10 locations)
  • Duplicate listings on Google/Yelp (6 instances)
  • Completely missing from Tier 1 platforms (3 locations)
  • Disconnected phone numbers (2 locations - outdated)

Tier 2: High Priority (Fix Weeks 3-4)

  • Business name inconsistencies (35+ locations)
  • Avenue vs Ave variations (40+ locations)
  • Missing from Tier 2 platforms (20+ locations)
  • Incomplete address data (suite numbers, postal codes)

Tier 3: Medium Priority (Fix Weeks 5-6)

  • Phone format standardization (30+ locations)
  • Tier 3 platform additions
  • Hours of operation updates
  • Description/category optimizations

REAL BUSINESS IMPACT EXAMPLES

Hamilton Dundurn Case Study

Situation: 50% NAP consistency (worst performer in my audit)

Specific Issues:

  • Listed as "Sessions Cannabis Hamilton" on some platforms, "Sessions Dundurn" on others
  • Missing from Weedmaps entirely (major traffic source)
  • Wrong phone number on Yelp (disconnected, causing negative reviews)
  • Address listed as "Hamilton" instead of specific neighborhood

Performance Impact:

  • Google local pack appearance: ~15% of time (vs 45% for well-optimized locations)
  • Customer complaints about "can't find you" (frustration → lost sales)
  • Lower review count (customers leaving reviews on wrong listing)

Estimated monthly revenue loss: $12,000-18,000 (vs properly optimized location)
